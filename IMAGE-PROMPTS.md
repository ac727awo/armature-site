# Armature Wealth Office — Image Generation Prompts

Three images. Each has a specific placement, function, and required visual language.
Use these prompts verbatim in Midjourney (v6.1+), Adobe Firefly (Generative Fill off), or DALL-E 3.
Do not add people, do not add text overlays, do not introduce colors outside the specified palette.

---

## IMAGE 1 — `governance-desk.jpg`
**Placement:** Homepage → "Why Clients Choose Armature" section (left column, portrait 4:5 ratio)
**Function:** Signals governed wealth, deliberate authorship, analogue authority. Replaces the abstract 3D cross render.

### Prompt

Studio editorial still life photograph. A large, aged mahogany writing desk photographed in portrait orientation (4:5 ratio). Surface partially visible — roughly two-thirds of the frame. Centered on the desk: a thick, hand-bound leather journal, cover closed, dark forest green with subtle gold embossed corner detailing, slight patina of age and use. Beside it, a single uncapped gold fountain pen resting diagonally across the bottom-right corner of the journal. To the upper-left: one folded linen document, cream-white, partially open, revealing a hand-drawn organizational hierarchy diagram — simple pencil lines, rectangular boxes connected by arrows, no labels readable. A small solid brass rectangular paperweight holds the document's left corner flat. No laptop. No phone. No coffee cup. No loose papers. Nothing decorative. Surfaces are dark mahogany with a deep matte grain visible. Background is a dark forest green wall, slightly out of focus. Lighting is natural window light entering from the upper-left, warm and directional, casting a single soft shadow to the right of the journal. Depth of field: shallow, with the journal sharp and the background wall soft. Film grain texture, shot on large-format film. Color palette strictly: deep forest green (#1c3828), aged cream (#f5f0eb), dark mahogany brown, brass/antique gold (#b8913a). No digital-looking elements. No people. No hands. Aspect ratio 4:5. Horizontal resolution minimum 2400px.

## IMAGE 2 — `integration-diagram.jpg`
**Placement:** Homepage → "Our Services" section (right column, portrait 3:4 ratio) AND Services page → "Integration over Isolation" section (left column, landscape 4:3 ratio)
**Function:** Signals system integration, whole-system thinking, coordinated oversight — not real estate or finance in a transactional sense. Replaces the green leather folder image.

### Prompt

Studio editorial still life photograph. A wide, dark oak writing table photographed in a slightly elevated three-quarter angle. The table surface dominates the lower two-thirds of the frame. Laid flat on the surface: one large open document folio — dark forest green leather cover folded back, interior cream-white linen pages visible. On the open pages, a cleanly hand-drawn diagram occupies the right page: six labeled rectangular boxes arranged in two rows of three, connected by thin pencil lines showing relationships between them — the boxes are labeled in small precise handwriting with words like "Portfolio," "Tax," "Estate," "Income," "Risk," "Continuity." The lines connecting them show interdependency, not hierarchy. A brass ruler sits along the bottom edge of the folio. One additional cream document, folded in half, rests to the upper-right of the folio. A small brass desk lamp enters the left frame edge, its warm cone of light illuminating the folio. Background: blurred dark mahogany bookshelves, horizontal book spines visible but not legible. No people. No laptop. No phone. No coffee. No decorative objects. Lighting: warm incandescent lamp light from the left, subtle cool daylight from a window at upper-right, creating a defined but soft shadow across the table. Film grain texture, large-format film aesthetic. Color palette strictly: deep forest green (#1c3828), cream (#f5f0eb), dark oak brown, aged brass (#b8913a), warm amber lamp glow. Aspect ratio: 4:3 (landscape). Horizontal resolution minimum 2400px.

## IMAGE 3 — `insights-editorial.jpg`
**Placement:** Insights page → top of each article card (full card width, landscape 16:9 or 3:2 ratio, displayed at approximately 380px wide × 200px tall)
**Function:** Signals a place of careful thought, not commerce. Draws the reader in before they read the headline. Should feel like a private reading room — calm, intelligent, analogue.

### Prompt

Wide editorial interior photograph of a private reading room or study, shot at approximately 3:2 ratio. Room is seen from a slight distance — not a close-up — so the full atmosphere of the space is visible. Dark mahogany bookshelves line the back wall, floor to ceiling, shelves filled with dark-spined books arranged neatly. In the foreground-left: a large dark wood desk, its surface mostly clear. On the desk: one open book, pages facing up, spine flat; beside it, a single folded document, cream-white; a small brass green-shaded banker's lamp is on, casting a warm amber cone of light across the book and document. No laptop. No phone. No coffee mug. No papers scattered. A leather-upholstered chair — dark forest green — is partially visible to the right of the desk, slightly pulled back, as if recently vacated. Tall narrow window in the back-right wall, natural light entering at a low afternoon angle, casting a long rectangle of warm light across the floor. The room is quiet, ordered, and serious. No people. No movement implied. Film grain texture, shot on 35mm film with slightly desaturated color treatment. Color palette strictly: deep forest green (#1c3828), warm cream (#f5f0eb), dark mahogany, aged brass, warm amber lamp light. No digital equipment visible anywhere in the frame. Aspect ratio 3:2. Horizontal resolution minimum 1800px.
